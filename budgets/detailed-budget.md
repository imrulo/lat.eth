## Logical Framework Structure

This budget is organized following the Logical Framework Matrix methodology, which structures the project into four hierarchical levels:

1. **GOAL (Development Objective):** Defend and preserve Latino identity through blockchain
2. **PURPOSE (Project Objective):** Create sustainable network of Latino digital identities
3. **COMPONENTS (Products/Results):** Infrastructure, personnel, technology and marketing
4. **ACTIVITIES (Specific Tasks):** Detailed breakdown of all tasks

## Cost Optimization for 20 Events

### Economies of Scale
1. **Venue Partnerships**: Long-term contracts reduce per-event costs
2. **Instructor Contracts**: Bulk booking reduces hourly rates
3. **Material Production**: Larger quantities reduce per-unit costs
4. **Equipment Sharing**: Reuse across multiple events
5. **Marketing Efficiency**: Campaigns cover multiple events

### Cost Reduction Strategies
- **Venue Partnerships**: Negotiate reduced rates for 20-event commitment
- **Instructor Collaboration**: Partner with local dance schools for reduced fees
- **Digital Materials**: Reduce printing costs by focusing on digital distribution
- **Volunteer Support**: Recruit community volunteers for non-technical roles
- **Equipment Sharing**: Partner with local venues for shared equipment use
